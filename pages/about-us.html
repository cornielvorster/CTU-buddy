About Us

CTU-Buddy is an educational system for City Technical University (CTU).

Vision: To provide a thorough and engaging website that provides students with knowledge, encourages a feeling of community, and supports academic success.

Mission: Establishing a user-friendly platform that offers a variety of courses, allows for easy contact between students and teachers, and promotes cooperation among learners will transform online learning.
