About Us

CTU-Buddy is an educational system for City Technical University (CTU).

Vision

To provide a thorough and engaging website that provides students with knowledge, encourages a feeling of community, and supports academic success.

Mission

Establishing a user-friendly platform that offers a variety of courses, allows for easy contact between students and teachers, and promotes cooperation among learners will transform online learning.

Our Purpose

The purpose of the CTU-Buddy website is to offer a centralized and innovative platform that enhances the learning experience for students at City Technical University. The website will serve as a hub where students can access various features and functionalities to manage their academic responsibilities effectively. CTU-Buddy aims to provide the following key benefits:

  • Improved time management with the help of the timetable management feature.
  • Collaborative learning through the discussion page.
  • Resource sharing is done through the resource page where users can upload PDF documents.
  • Giving direct communication to students for feedback about the website using the contact us page.
