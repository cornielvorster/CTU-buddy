About Us

CTU-Buddy is an innovative educational system developed for the students of City Technical University (CTU). Our mission is to enhance the learning experience and provide valuable resources to help students excel in their academic journey.

Vision: To create a comprehensive and interactive website that empowers students with knowledge, fosters a sense of community, and promotes academic success.

Mission: To revolutionize online learning by offering a user-friendly platform that offers a wide range of courses, facilitates seamless communication between students and educators, and encourages collaboration among learners.
